the challenge

Stories are the nourishment of a society. How a society thinks, behaves, and functions, is directly tied to the stories it consumes.

A society aspiring to a state of wellness for all needs to start with telling healthy stories. Unfortunately, our stories have become compromised. Media is saturated with stories of crime, regressive morality and violence, catering to our worst impulses. We are overcome in a sea of junk culture that erodes our intellectual health, our emotional wellness and our potential of achieving long term wellness for humanity. Not too long ago, we witnessed something similar in the food industry. As junk food disrupted the health of billions of people, the health food industry boomed, pushing back with a better alternative.

As a mass-media studio battling the same primal human instincts, we are invested in the question: What is the avocado of cinema and what is the yoga of games?

the solution

impact

We produce awe-inspiring stories that further the ideals of social justice and equity. All our media is a direct exploration and reflection of our core value - an equal, just world is the foundation of sustainable progress.

As a studio, we champion the most progressive ideas of the zeitgeist and make them attractive and palatable for audiences around the world.

We aim to generate exponential intellectual and cultural wealth, that provide new frameworks to live and grow in, and help interpret our world better.
